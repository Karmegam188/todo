Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Missile Man of India"

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he played a pivotal role in India's civilian space and military missile development efforts.

He was known for his humility, vision for a developed India, and dedication to education and youth empowerment. He authored several books including *Wings of Fire* and *Ignited Minds* that continue to inspire generations.

Dr. Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, while delivering a lecture. His life remains a guiding light for aspiring scientists and citizens of India.
